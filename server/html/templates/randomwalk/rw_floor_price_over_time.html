Floor Price Over Time

Random Walk Token Address {{.RWalkAddr}}
MarketPlace Address {{.MarketAddr}}
Period length {{.Interval}} seconds


Shows floor price, calculated (as a MIN() function) in intervals of {{.Interval}} seconds




API: /black/rwalk/statistics/floor_price/:market_addr/:rwalk_addr/:init_ts/:fin_ts/:interval_secs

Example: /black/rwalk/statistics/floor_price/{{.MarketAddr}}/{{.RWalkAddr}}/{{.InitTs}}/{{.FinTs}}/{{.Interval}}
