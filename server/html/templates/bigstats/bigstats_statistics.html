Legend

  • Unique Human - unique active addresses (addresses that made at least 1 transaction) sent by human accounts (EOA type account) within 24 hours
  • Unique Contract - unique active addresses (addresses that were involved in at least 1 transaction) that belong to a contract account (single transaction can make accesss to few of such contract accounts) within 24 hours
  • Transferred - amount in ETH that was transferred in 24 hours between accounts of different types (contract or human)
  • Tx Fees in ETH - the transaction fee in ETH
  • Tx Fees in US Dollars, calculated per individual transaction using price of ETH averaged within interval of 30 minutes
